Shandong Tuoguangtong Information Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the development, production, and sales of communication network equipment.

The company focuses on providing domestic and international telecom operators, telecom equipment manufacturers, and network integrators with first-class communication network connection, distribution, and protection products and overall solutions.

Main products include: broadband main distribution frames (MDF), digital distribution frames (DDF), optical fiber management equipment (ODF), FTTX, outdoor equipment rooms, outdoor cabinets, network cabinets, broadband and narrowband integrated cabling systems, power distribution systems, cable tray management systems, intelligent ventilation systems, and mobile communication antenna systems.

With its excellent performance and superior quality, the company’s products have been operating stably and extensively in the communication networks of major operators and private network users for many years. The product range comprehensively covers large and medium-sized central equipment rooms at the central office level and small-scale distribution equipment at the user end.
